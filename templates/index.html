About Aklima

With 4 years of hands‑on clinical experience, Aklima Munaf Shaikh, PT has helped patients of all ages come back from injury, surgery, and chronic pain. Her approach blends scientific physiotherapy techniques with empathy, patience, and clear communication.

She believes that every body is unique. That’s why each treatment plan is tailored to your lifestyle, goals, and comfort level – whether you’re an office professional, homemaker, athlete, or senior citizen.

  • Thorough 1:1 assessment and goal setting
  • Gentle, progressive exercises designed for you
  • Education on posture, ergonomics, and self‑care

Services

Pain Relief & Joint Care

Targeted manual therapy, stretching, and strengthening exercises to relieve neck, back, knee, and shoulder pain.

Post‑Surgery Rehabilitation

Safe, guided recovery after knee, hip, spine, or shoulder surgeries to restore strength and mobility step‑by‑step.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Tailored therapy for stroke, nerve injuries, and balance problems to improve coordination, strength, and independence.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Gentle, play‑based rehab for babies and children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, or post‑injury needs.

Her Treatment Approach

  1. 1. Detailed assessment of pain, movement, and lifestyle.
  2. 2. Clear explanation of your condition in simple language.
  3. 3. A personalized plan combining hands‑on therapy and exercises.
  4. 4. Regular progress tracking and gentle progression.
